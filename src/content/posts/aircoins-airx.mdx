<p><em>14 May 2021</em></p><p><a href="https://aircoins.io/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Aircoins.io</a></p><p>Aircoins describes itself as an augmented reality, cryptocurrency gaming, and marketing App available on Android and IOS App stores. Users can hunt for and collect cryptocurrencies within an interactive AR environment based on geolocation similar to Pokemon Go. The Aircoins team claims to have partnered with over 25 crypto projects, acquiring 27,000 active users with over 13M coins collected within the platform.</p>
